🎯 MISSION
────────────────────────────────────

Brain Buds is designed to help students:
• Test their knowledge through quizzes
• Identify weak areas for improvement
• Build consistent learning habits
• Compete and learn in a fun way

🎮 QUIZ MODES EXPLAINED
────────────────────────────────────

Quick Quiz (5 Qs, 60 sec):
• Fast testing
• Best for revision

Full Test (20 Qs, 10 min):
• Complete topic coverage
• Best for thorough practice

Practice Mode (10 Qs, No timer):
• No pressure
• Learn at your own pace
• See answers immediately

Exam Mode (15 Qs, 5 min):
• Real exam simulation
• Negative marking (-2 for wrong)
• Time pressure
